ShortSpike Cutting Routine


Overview

This is a cutting routine that I am implementing from 07/12/15 to 08/01/15. This is in prep for a men's physique contest. The primary goal is to lose body fat. The secondary goal is to keep as much muscle as possible.

Conclusions

This routine appears to be effective in long-term cutting while maintaining muscle. It is not an effective diet to see results in a short period of time. It might take a very long time to get a competition cut on this diet, if at all. So this diet is recommended as a maintenance diet. Further testing will see how effective this diet is over a longer period of time.

The format is as follows

  • The pictures mark the end of a recording period. They are the deciding factor to whether or not the diet works
  • The data recorded for each day are the things I have done from the last pictures to the current ones
  • Pictures are recorded before a workout
  • So working backwards from the time of the picture, the relevant data is recorded
  • For example, I take a pre-workout just before the pictures
  • The next relevant information is yesterday's diet and workout
  • Then during the workout that followed the last pictures I take an Intra-Workout, which is basically just BCAA's
  • Although Tobacco is clearly unhealthy, I use it to help curb cravings. I try my best not to use it especially when not cutting, but during cutting I allow it
