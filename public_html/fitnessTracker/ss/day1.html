This was the last day of my Saw Blade Lean Muscle Builder Plan. I didn't record any data for this one. I'm looking forward to doing this plan after the show and recording the data. That plan starts off high with a cheat meal day, gradually descends to a mid-lean day, and the third day is a full lean day. Then it sharply jumps up to a cheat meal day and repeats. So the shape of the calorie intake is like a saw blade. It's supposed to keep your hormones and metabolism from adjusting to the reduced calorie days so you still burn fat while gaining some muscle
